**📌 Objective**
    Vrinda Store wants to create an annual sales report for 2022 to understand customer behavior and improve sales strategies for 2023.

**🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used**
    Microsoft Excel
    Pivot Tables
    Pivot Charts
    Data Cleaning
    Data Visualization
    Formulas (IF, SUM, COUNT, etc.)

**📊 Key Business Questions Solved**
    Compare Sales vs Orders using a single chart
    Identify the month with highest sales and orders
    Analyze gender-wise purchasing behavior (Men vs Women)
    Understand order status distribution (Delivered, Cancelled, etc.)
    Find Top 10 states contributing to sales
    Analyze relation between Age & Gender based on orders
    Identify sales contribution by channels (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.)
    Determine the highest selling product category

**📈 Dashboard Features**
    Interactive dashboard with filters
    Sales vs Orders comparison chart
    Monthly performance analysis
    Gender-based insights
    State-wise top contributors
    Channel performance visualization
    Age group segmentation

**🔍 Key Insights**
    👩 Women contribute ~65% of total purchases
    📍 Top 3 states: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh (~35%)
    👨‍👩‍👧 Adult age group (30–49 years) contributes ~50%
    🛒 Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra contribute ~80% of sales

📌 **Final Conclusion**
    To increase sales in 2023, Vrinda Store should:
      🎯 Target women customers (age 30–49 years)
      📍 Focus on Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh
      📢 Promote products via Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra
      💰 Use ads, offers, and coupons for better engagement

**🚀 How to Use**
    Download the Excel file
    Open in Microsoft Excel
    Use filters/slicers to explore insights
